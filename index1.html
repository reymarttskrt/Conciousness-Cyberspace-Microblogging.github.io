About Us

Researchers From 12 ICT 2

In the Philippines, there is a huge cybercrime rate that has been recorded on June 2021 by Statista. So as students, we want to help to reduce the percentage of this type of crime by creating a website that will provide useful information on how to avoid being a victim of those type of illegal activities that occurs in cyberspace. The developers are from Arellano University Jose Abad Santos who aim to increase the awareness of cybercrime among teachers and students. This microblogging is significant because the use of the internet nowadays is prevalent due to its helpfulness to every individual. However, some of them use this to commit crimes. So, with the assistance of this website, the user's understanding of the various types of cybercrime will be enhanced.
